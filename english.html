So how does coaching work?

Firstly, we will arrange a call (video call will be best), where we can talk about your challenges, goals and wishes for your home. You can get to know me a little in the call and we can establish if we would like to work with each other.

Secondly, we determine a date, when I will visit your house to collaborate with you on your organizing project. Depending on the project size (one cupboard, a whole room or entire flat) we will need at least one or more organizing sessions. Decluttering and organizing will start! I will implement a new structure that works for you and we will establish order in your home.

If you don’t live in Berlin or a personal session is not possible, I can also coach you via video-call.
Are you also interested in interior design?
Should you want to change one room or to redesign your whole home? I can happily consult you regarding the furnishing and decorating of your home providing you with new ideas, perspective and proposals.
